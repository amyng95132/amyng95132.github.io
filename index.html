About Amy

Johor Bahru, my birthplace, is located on the peninsular of West Malaysia. I was born in this charming fishing village that sits next to the equator.

Since I was a kid, my dad and I have enjoyed drawing, swimming, hiking, and playing music. Piano is my primary instrument and flute is my secondary instrument. Ah! You don't want to hear me play the flute :)

My dad is my art teacher. He is also my English teacher. We love to venture around the old town and visit the bookstores.

We both agreed that the best bookstore was owned by an elderly Indian gentleman in my hometown. This historical bookstore was a 2 stories building and both sides of the old wooden stair were packed with books. Frankly, it's the grandest staircase I've ever seen in my life.
