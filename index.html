About Amy

Johor Bahru, my birthplace, is located on the peninsular of West Malaysia. I was born in this a charming fishing village that sits next to the equator.

Since I was a kid, I enjoy drawing and playing music. Piano is my primary instrument and flute is my secondary instrument. Ah! You don't want to hear me play the flute :)

My dad is my one and only drawing teacher. He is also my English teacher. We love to venture around the old town and visit the bookstores. The best bookstore was owned by an elder Indian gentleman. The bookstore is a 2 stories building and both sides of the old wooden stair were packed with books. Frankly, it's the grandest stair I ever seen in my life.
