Bonn, a forgotten town, near the Rhine River. In the middle of a quiet lane, the humble two stories building is Beethoven's house. I stepped in and heard the cracking of the wooden floor.

Shouldn't Beethoven's house deserve the same popularity as Mozart's house in Salzburg?

Beethoven, a passionate composer, was an early bird who loved coffee. Oh, who doesn't like a good cup of coffee? The aroma of coffee brighten my morning.
