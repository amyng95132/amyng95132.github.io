Bonn, a forgotten town, near the Rhine River. In the middle of a quiet lane, the humble two stories building is Beethoven's house. I stepped in and heard the creaking of the wooden floor.

There was a replica of Beethoven's piano in the hall. At the back of the house, there was a charming garden. Next to the garden, there is a lab with some computers. In the lab, I had a great time playing the games and learning about Beethoven.

Beethoven, a passionate composer, was an early bird who loved coffee. Oh, who doesn't like a good cup of coffee? The sweet scent of coffee brighten my morning.

I am inspired to design a simple 3 questions game about music history. Now, grap a cup of good coffee and start the game. Have fun!
